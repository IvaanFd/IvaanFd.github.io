Personal Finance and Introduction to Investment

This initiative proposes an accessible and practical conference on financial management, personal finance, and investment basics, designed for employees of all ages and career stages. Its purpose is to raise awareness about the importance of building solid financial health and to provide practical tools to plan and make informed financial decisions.

Overview

The conference addresses the financial challenges many individuals face, especially early in their careers, due to the lack of formal education on topics like saving, investing, and long-term planning. Participants will gain essential knowledge to build a strong financial foundation and better manage their resources.

Main Topics

  • Savings and Investments: Introduction to funds, stocks, fixed income, and strategies to start investing with limited capital.
  • Long-term Planning: Building an emergency fund and strategies for goals like retirement.
  • Common Mistakes and Financial Habits: How to avoid frequent errors and develop healthy financial habits.
  • Basic Concepts: Inflation, pension systems, and principles for building and protecting personal wealth.
  • Personal Budget: The 50-30-20 rule for organizing income and expenses efficiently.

Presentation Structure

  1. Why is Investing Necessary? The relationship between investment, inflation, and financial stability.
  2. What is the Key to Investing? Financial priorities: budgeting, emergency funds, debt elimination, and automating investments.
  3. What Investment Vehicles Exist? Explanation of options like fixed income, ETFs, index funds, and how to choose reliable platforms.
  4. What is My Strategy? Practical example of financial organization and application of the concepts covered in the conference.

Main Objective

The goal is to provide participants with practical tools and actionable knowledge to better manage their money, invest wisely, and build a solid financial foundation for the future. This initiative also aims to align financial well-being strategies with organizational goals.

Key Benefits

  • Accessibility: Designed for everyone, regardless of prior knowledge.
  • Practical Application: Participants will be able to implement the concepts learned in their daily lives.
  • Well-being Culture: Promotes personal and professional growth among employees.

Approach

The presentation will be designed to be clear, simple, and accessible, ensuring that all attendees, regardless of their knowledge level, can follow and apply the concepts in their daily lives. My personal experience with financial management will serve as a foundation to illustrate these concepts.
